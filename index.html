what it's about

the most satisfying and fulfilling moments in my life have been in the mountains and forests of the northeast and pacific northwest. though time there is limited, the moments spent in the evergreen shade or summit panorama are the ones i come back to remember most often.

things that keep me stoked:

  • outdoor flow sports
    • rock climbing
    • mountain biking
    • skiing, telemark, paragliding, and backcountry touring
  • really great single origin coffee and espresso
  • socialism, politics, and philosophy
  • music, guitar, seeing live shows
  • i don't code much recreationally, but when i do, it goes on github.

this site, like myself, is a work in progress and will be updated when i feel like it. for now, this is where i've gotten.
